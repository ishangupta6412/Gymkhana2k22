MANIFESTO


General Facilities

High-Speed Internet Across Campus

Imagine the sad state of affairs our college is in right now that even in 2022 we must keep high-speed internet, a necessity in our manifesto. We promise that access to high-speed internet will be our first order of business once we come into position.

Introduction of new fests and student activities

For an institute of national importance, little to no focus is put on the celebration of regional festivals, the diversity, that unites us. We promise that a more inclusive Gymkhana will be formed which will not only give you the cultural activities which you deserve but also a new Sports Fest.

Laundry and Printing services in each hostel.

The printing services in the college are not easily accessible. This often leads to excessive crowding and a lot of time gets wasted. To solve this issue, more printing facilities will be made available on campus. In addition, provisions will also be made so that getting your laundry done is much easier.

Reduction of auto fares to standard pricing.

Travelling away from NIT Durgapur can be quite an expensive deal given the inflated auto prices. We promise a standardised rate of autos or will ensure that at least shared-auto services resume in front of the main gate. In addition, we also promise to start a bicycle service on the college campus to facilitate going out.


Academic Facilities

24x7 Library with improved facilities

A 24x7 Library has often been promised by the hollow administration, but it has never been met. A 24x7 Library and access to industry-level softwares are the birthright of every engineer. We promise that this birthright of ours shall be ensured. Furthermore, services like LAN cables, and an increased number of seating arrangements will be made available for the students.

Mentorship and mock interviews for placements

Even after being adept with the right technical skills, many students feel out of place at interviews. This is widely due to lack of proper communication skills and interview experiences. In view of the same, we guarantee that mock interviews will be held and professional help will be provided in communication skills regard, to those who need it.

Improved infrastructure for placements & internships.

Outside of the tech world is a whole new universe of many different opportunities like consulting, finance, banking, etc. If allowed to serve, we promise to bring these opportunities in their primary roles to NIT Durgapur, therefore allowing students to fit in whatever size fits them best. In addition, smoother flow of information will be made accessible. To achieve this a dedicated online portal with proper guidance will be made for the students to keep them updated.


Sports, Healthcare, and Cultural Facilities

New and improved equipment in the gymnasium.

A gym is not just a place where we lift weights, we lift weights, and tend to forget the other worries of this world. And to see it into the mess it has become, only motivates us to let it be one of our first worries. We’ll thus ensure proper maintenance of the gym facilities, introduction of better equipments and also more flexible gym timings.

Improved Sports Facilities with emphasis on Inter-NIT

The sports facilities in the college are such that even after putting tremendous efforts into their individual selves, our teams have not been able to perform well in Inter-NIT competitions. Once we come into the position, we assure you that proper maintenance with special emphasis on Ovals, Lords, and their neighbouring courts will occur along with unrestrained timings. In addition, we also promise better opportunities for students who find their interest in other sports.

Ameliorated Medical Unit

To ameliorate means to make something bad or unsatisfactory better. The condition of the Medical Unit is such that just in the last semester, a student was almost on the verge of death due to negligence from the MU. Even after such incidents, the administration has done little to nothing to make things better. In view of the same, stringent measures will be taken into ameliorating the medical unit. In addition, efforts are already being made for a medical store to be made available on the campus.

More opportunities for student representation at various levels.

What is a gymkhana if students feel like their voices are not being heard? To ensure this a more inclusive gymkhana where students can register their complaints on a redressal portal shall be made.

In addition, our college’s involvement in extracurricular activities outside of the college is very limited. This often makes NIT Durgapur a confined space with not much extraterrestrial growth. We will ensure that the students get more opportunities to showcase their talents in fields like gaming, debating, and cultural activities like dancing, singing, etc at their best.


Essential Facilities

Convenience Store for Women

The ladies of the college have to often wander to get basic necessities like sanitary needs, vital requirements that should be available on the campus. In view of the same, we want to make proper arrangements for a women-centred convenience store, something more like a “Women’s Techno”.

Night Canteen to be open till 4 AM

Engineering students function differently than others in the sense that they often stay up late at night to keep on track with everything. During this time, there are no arrangements for food. When brought into position, we will make provisions for a night canteen which will serve everyone till late at night.

Initiation of Shopping Complex services

The college has been unable to provide tenders to shop owners in the shopping complex. We will ensure that tenders are issued and shops are opened in the shopping complex, which will further take care of issues like convenience stores, night canteens, etc.

Improved Mess Food quality

For an extended period of time, residents of Halls which have private messes have been constantly unsatisfied with their food. Serious measures will be taken to elevate the quality. And if need be, government messes shall also be reintroduced in these Halls.
